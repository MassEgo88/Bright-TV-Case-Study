## Project Overview

Bright TV is a digital streaming platform experiencing uneven user engagement across different days, regions, and audience segments.

This project uses data analysis to uncover **consumption patterns**, identify **low-engagement gaps**, and recommend **strategies to increase user engagement and platform growth**.

---

##  Business Problem

Bright TV faces **low engagement on certain weekdays and in underperforming regions**, limiting overall growth and retention.

Understanding **when, where, and how users consume content** is critical to improving engagement and expanding the user base.

---

## Objectives

* Analyse consumption trends by **day, time, and region**
* Understand user behaviour across **age and gender segments**
* Identify **low-consumption periods and underserved markets**
* Provide **data-driven recommendations** to improve engagement and growth

---

## Methodology

* Cleaned and transformed raw data using **SQL** (joins, aggregations, null handling)
* Analysed consumption trends across **time, region, and demographics**
* Built interactive dashboards in **Power BI** to visualise key insights
* Interpreted patterns to generate **business-focused recommendations**

---

## Key Insights

### Consumption Trends

* Engagement **peaks on Fridays** and drops on **Tuesdays and Wednesdays**
* Weekdays contribute ~60% of total consumption, but **midweek engagement is weak**
* **Afternoon and prime-time** are the most active viewing periods

**Insight:** Users show **weekend-biased viewing behaviour**, creating an opportunity to improve weekday engagement.

---

### User Behaviour

* Ages **25–34 drive ~50% of total engagement**
* Ages **18–24 follow**, consuming more youth-focused content
* **Male users dominate engagement (86%)**, with significantly lower female participation

 **Insight:** There is a **major gender engagement gap**, indicating a content and targeting opportunity.

---

### Regional Performance

* **Gauteng leads** in overall consumption
* **North West, Free State, Northern Cape, and unknown regions** show low engagement

**Insight:** These regions represent **high-growth opportunities** if properly targeted.

---

## Key Opportunities

* Improve **weekday engagement (especially midweek)**
* Increase **female user participation**
* Expand into **underperforming provinces**

---

## Recommendations

### Increasing Engagement

* **Content Scheduling:** Release premium content on low-activity days (e.g. Tuesdays)
* **Push Notifications:** Target users during off-peak periods
* **Personalised Recommendations:** Suggest content based on viewing behaviour
* **Gamification:** Introduce watch streaks, rewards, and badges
* **Promotions:** Offer discounted or free data viewing during low-engagement periods

---

### Growth Strategy

#### Acquire New Users

* Partner with telecom providers for **data + streaming bundles**
* Pre-install Bright TV on **affordable smartphones**
* Launch **referral programs** to drive organic growth
* Invest in **local and regional content**
* Use **short-form content on social media** to attract new users

#### Retain & Grow Existing Users

* Improve onboarding with **personalised recommendations**
* Implement **AI-driven content suggestions**
* Encourage habit formation through **scheduled content releases**

---

## Expected Impact

* Increase weekday engagement by **15–25%**
* Improve **user retention and session duration**
* Grow user base in **underserved regions**
