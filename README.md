## Project Overview

This project presents a data-driven analysis of Bright TV’s user consumption patterns, with the goal of uncovering actionable insights to support the Customer Value Management team in driving growth.

The analysis focuses on understanding **user behavior, regional trends, and content performance** to recommend strategies that increase engagement and expand the user base.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze user consumption trends across different time periods
* Identify high and low engagement patterns
* Understand regional (provincial) performance differences
* Provide recommendations to increase overall platform usage
* Suggest growth initiatives to attract and retain users

## Key Insights

* **Consumption Trends:** Clear patterns show peak usage during specific days, with noticeable drop-offs that present opportunities for targeted engagement strategies.
* **User Behavior:** Certain content types consistently drive higher engagement, indicating strong audience preferences.
* **Regional Insights:** Variations across provinces highlight where growth efforts should be focused.
* **Engagement Gaps:** Low-consumption days suggest opportunities for promotions, content drops, or targeted campaigns.

---

## Recommendations

* Introduce **targeted content releases** on low-engagement days
* Use **personalized recommendations** to increase watch time
* Launch **regional campaigns** in underperforming provinces
* Implement **loyalty or reward programs** to retain active users
* Optimize content strategy based on high-performing categories
