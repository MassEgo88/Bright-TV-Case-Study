Fixing Low-Day Consumption
01
Strategic Content Drops
Release premium episodes or trending content on Tuesdays and Wednesdays to drive viewership on the lowest-traffic days. Example: "New episode drops every Tuesday."
Quick Win
02
Targeted Push Notifications
Send personalized "Don't miss out" alerts during low-activity windows. Time notifications to match each user's peak viewing hour for maximum open rates.
Quick Win
03
Personalized Recommendations
Use viewing history to surface relevant content. "Because you watched…" style suggestions increase session time and reduce churn during low-engagement periods.
Mid-term
04
Gamification & Rewards
Introduce watch streaks, badges, and loyalty points to build habitual viewing behaviour. Users who maintain streaks are significantly less likely to churn.
Mid-term
05
Free Data Hours / Promotions
Offer zero-rated streaming windows during low-traffic hours (e.g., Tuesday nights). This is particularly effective in the South African market where data cost is a barrier.
Quick Win
06
Female-Focused Content
With female viewership at only 12%, investing in lifestyle, drama, and reality content creates a major new audience segment. Target female users during afternoon peak hours.
Strategic
Growing the User Base
07
Telecom Partnerships
Bundle Bright TV with mobile data packages (MTN, Vodacom, Telkom). Device pre-installs on entry-level Android phones can unlock the low-consumption provinces.
Strategic
08
Referral Programme
"Invite a friend, earn rewards" — word-of-mouth is the highest-converting acquisition channel in SA. Tie rewards to data vouchers or extended free access.
Mid-term
09
Local & Regional Content
Invest in shows from low-consumption provinces (North West, Free State). Local language content is the single biggest driver of new user acquisition in SA streaming markets.
Strategic
10
Social Media Clips
Distribute short clips on TikTok, Instagram Reels, and Facebook to drive app installs. The 18–24 age group (2nd largest segment) is primarily reachable through short-form social content.
Quick Win
11
Better Onboarding
First-day recommendations dramatically impact 30-day retention. Show new users 5 personalised picks immediately after sign-up based on their age and location.
Mid-term
12
Habit-Building Scheduling
Daily or weekly episodic shows create routine viewing patterns. A 5-night serialised drama turns casual viewers into loyal subscribers.
